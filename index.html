When evaluating a job offer / company my checklist is:

  • Offers a decent salary
  • Allows to work on other projects while under contract
  • Gives back to and / or does Open Source / OSS
  • Dev team led by CTO
  • Remote work
  • Flexible hours
  • Learning budget (training / conferences)
  • Offers lunch w/ team (if not remote)
  • Offers coworking space allowance (if remote)
  • Offers equipment (hardware / software)

You might want to try it before sending me an offer!
